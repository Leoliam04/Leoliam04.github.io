Tank Man Enters The Maze

Tank Man Enters The Maze is third/first person adventure game in which you play a robot stowaway who explores alien ruins.

Plane Testing

Plane Testing was an experiment in creating working planes in unity with physics. Some physics, however started creating issues and AI was difficult to implement.

Water Physics

Water Physics is a test created to explore the possiblilites involving water in games. In it you can control a large submarine, battleship, small submarine, small boat and a tanker. All seamlessly in a world built around water.
